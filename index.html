An optical poem produced by Oskar Fischinger 1938 is a film about geometric shapes. When the film starts you see a few spheres, the music starts slowly. When you keep watching, the shapes become more and more and move even faster. The music changes in speed together with the shapes and even though the film includes only music and geometric shapes you keep watching. An optical poem is almsot working hypnotic

As a graphic designer we work a lot with geometric shapes. I believe that an optical poem an big inspiration bron is to graphic designers At least, the challenge to work with less elements and still make an inspirational work is very interesting to me.
